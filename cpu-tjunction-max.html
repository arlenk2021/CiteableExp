CPU Tjunction max (Tjmax)

Maximum CPU Tjunction varies by model; many AMD processors cap near 95 °C (https://www.amd.com/en/support/kb/faq/cpu-maximum-temperatures), while many Intel chips cite ~100 °C (https://www.intel.com/content/www/us/en/support/articles/000005597/processors.html).
